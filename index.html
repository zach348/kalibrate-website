How It Works

Kalibrate guides you through a structured session designed to capture your attention, engage your focus, and help you wind down naturally.

1. Check In

Begin with a brief assessment of your current stress and energy levels to personalize the session.

2. Focus Training

Track moving targets in an adaptive game that loads working memory and pulls attention away from rumination.

3. Rhythmic Guidance

Coordinated visual, haptic, and audio pulses help capture your focus and gradually slow your nervous system.

4. Guided Breathing

Research-backed breathing exercises shift your state from alert focus to calm regulation.

5. Track Progress

Review your session results and see how your focus and calm improve over time with consistent practice.

6. Adaptive Experience

Each session adapts to your current state and performance, keeping you in the optimal zone between boredom and anxiety.
